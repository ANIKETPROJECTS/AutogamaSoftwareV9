PART 1: Create Meta Business Account


Step 1: Go to business.facebook.com

Step 2: Click "Create Account" or log in if you already have one

Step 3: Enter your business details:

Business name: 
Your garage name
Your name
Business email


Step 4: Verify your business (Meta may ask for business documents)



PART 2: Create Meta Developer App


Step 1: Go to developers.facebook.com

Step 2: Click "My Apps" → "Create App"

Step 3: Select app type: "Business"

Step 4: Enter app details:

App name: "AutoGarage CRM"
Contact email: Your email
Business Account: Select your business
Step 5: Click "Create App"



PART 3: Add WhatsApp to Your App

Step 1: In your app dashboard, find "Add Products"

Step 2: Find "WhatsApp" and click "Set Up"

Step 3: Complete the Embedded Signup:

Connect your Facebook Business Account
Add a phone number for WhatsApp Business
Verify the phone number via SMS/call
Step 4: After setup, you'll see:

Your Phone Number ID (copy this)
Your WhatsApp Business Account ID (copy this)



PART 4: Generate Access Token

Step 1: Go to business.facebook.com → Settings

Step 2: Click "Users" → "System Users"

Step 3: Click "Add" to create a new system user:

Name: "CRM Bot"
Role: Admin
Step 4: Click on the user → "Add Assets" → Select your WhatsApp Business Account


Step 5: Click "Generate New Token":

Select your app
Check permission: whatsapp_business_messaging
Click "Generate Token"
Step 6: Copy the token immediately (it won't show again!)



PART 5: Create Message Templates

Step 1: Go to business.facebook.com → WhatsApp Manager

Step 2: Click "Message Templates" → "Create Template"

Step 3: Create these 6 templates:

Template Name	              Category	                    Message
service_new_lead	      Utility	                    Welcome! Your {{1}} ({{2}}) has been registered. We will contact you shortly.
service_inspection_done	      Utility	                    Inspection completed for your {{1}} ({{2}}). Our team will share the report soon.
service_work_in_progress      Utility	                    Work has started on your {{1}} ({{2}}). We will keep you updated.
service_ready_for_delivery    Utility	                    Great news! Your {{1}} ({{2}}) is ready for pickup. Please visit us at your convenience.
service_completed	      Utility	                    Thank you for choosing us! Service completed for your {{1}} ({{2}}). We hope to see you again!
service_cancelled	      Utility	                    Your service request for {{1}} ({{2}}) has been cancelled. Contact us for any queries.
Step 4: Submit each template for approval (takes 1-5 days)



PART 6: Provide Credentials to Me

Once you have everything ready, provide me these 3 secrets:

Secret Name	                               What It Is
WHATSAPP_ACCESS_TOKEN	                       The token you generated in Part 4, Step 6
WHATSAPP_PHONE_NUMBER_ID	               Found in WhatsApp Settings (Part 3, Step 4)
WHATSAPP_BUSINESS_ACCOUNT_ID	               Found in WhatsApp Settings (Part 3, Step 4)


What Happens After You Provide Credentials
I'll add them as secrets to your app
I'll update the WhatsApp code to use the real API
When you change service status in the funnel → WhatsApp message automatically sends to customer!
